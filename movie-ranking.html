The Best K-Drama by Poornima

My top 3 K-Dramas of all time.

True Beauty

It's not only because I like Hwang In Youp character,but also it describes how a man can make a women feel better about her insecurities. All that matters for a women is his man is adoring her and caring about her.

When Life Gives You Tangerines

This drama is the best I would say according to me, as the real thing what women want from a men and obviously it's not money.

Business Proposal

Basically I like romance and funny genre, this drama has both.This drama indicates a love beyond money and perfect humor for the audience.
